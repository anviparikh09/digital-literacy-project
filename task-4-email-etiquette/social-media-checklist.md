# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Think before you post anything  
- Be polite and respectful  
- Keep your account private and secure  
- Share useful or positive things  
- Use social media in a responsible way  

## Don’ts
- Don’t share personal details online  
- Don’t post anything offensive  
- Don’t believe or spread fake news  
- Don’t get into online fights  
- Don’t post things that can affect your future
