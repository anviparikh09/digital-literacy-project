# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank password with anyone.  
2. If you get a call or message from the bank, check with the bank first before doing anything.  
3. Don’t click on links in messages or emails from unknown people.  
4. Keep your phone and apps updated.  
5. Use strong passwords and turn on two-step verification.  
6. Check your bank account and UPI transactions regularly for anything unusual.  
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments.  
8. If anything happens, report it to the [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in) or call **1930**.
