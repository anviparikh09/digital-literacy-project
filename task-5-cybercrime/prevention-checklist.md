# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank password with anyone.  
2. Always verify unexpected calls or messages by contacting your bank directly.  
3. Avoid clicking on links from unknown sources in emails, SMS, or social media.  
4. Keep your mobile phone and payment apps updated with the latest security patches.  
5. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication.  
6. Check your bank and UPI transaction history regularly for unusual or unauthorized activity.  
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or making payments.  
8. If you notice suspicious activity or become a victim, report immediately to the [National Cyber Crime Portal](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline **1930**.  .
