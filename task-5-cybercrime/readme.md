# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

## Overview
This task helps students understand online crimes and how to stay safe. It includes a real-life style case study and a checklist with tips.

## Files Included
- [casestudy.md](./casestudy.md) – Shows a case of UPI/online payment fraud, how it happens, and who is targeted.  
- [prevention-checklist.md](./prevention-checklist.md) – Checklist with practical tips to avoid online fraud, including financial safety tips.  

## Instructions
1. Read the case study to understand UPI/online payment fraud.  
2. Follow the checklist to protect yourself while using online payments.  
3. Use these files to educate your friends and keep your online accounts safe.
