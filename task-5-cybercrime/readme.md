# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

## Overview
This task helps students understand online crimes and how to protect themselves. It includes a detailed case study and a checklist with practical prevention tips.

## Files Included
- [casestudy.md](./casestudy.md) – Detailed case study of UPI/online payment fraud with steps, targets, and consequences.  
- [prevention-checklist.md](./prevention-checklist.md) – Checklist of at least 8 safety tips, including financial safety measures.

## Instructions
1. Read the case study to understand how UPI/online payment fraud happens.  
2. Follow the checklist to implement safe online habits.  
3. Use these files to educate peers and maintain safe digital practices.
