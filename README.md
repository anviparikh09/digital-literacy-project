## Project Overview
This project is part of the Digital Literacy course. It focuses on developing essential digital skills required for students in today's technology-driven world. The project helps in understanding online communication, creating a professional digital presence, and promoting safe and responsible internet usage.

---

## Tasks Summary

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
An infographic was created using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. It covers important topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence.

### Task 2: Digital Portfolio
A digital portfolio was built by creating profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in showcasing skills, projects, and professional growth over time.

### Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
A beginner-level coding challenge was completed on a coding platform to improve problem-solving skills. Additionally, a Google Form quiz was created to spread awareness about digital literacy among students.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
Two professional emails were drafted — one to a professor requesting an extension and another to an internship coordinator. A checklist of social media do’s and don’ts was also created to encourage responsible online behavior.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on cybercrime was prepared to understand common online threats. A prevention checklist was also created to help students stay safe online and avoid digital fraud.
